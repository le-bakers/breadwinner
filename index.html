See how it works

Three steps, no squinting required

  1. 1

    Photograph or upload

    Use your camera or choose a photo of your paper receipt. Good lighting helps, but Breadwinner handles a little glare or crinkle just fine.

  2. 2

    We read every line

    Breadwinner checks each item against known gluten-containing ingredients and flags certified or labeled gluten-free specialty products.

  3. 3

    Get your totals

    See what to avoid, what's safe, and your tax-deductible gluten-free total — ready to save, print, or share with your accountant.
