01Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how BreadWinner ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and Service. Because BreadWinner deals with grocery receipts tied to a medical condition — celiac disease and gluten sensitivity — we treat this data with particular care, even where it does not meet the legal definition of health information in your jurisdiction.

02Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information — name, email address, and password (stored as a salted hash, never in plain text)
  • Receipt content — photos or uploads of grocery receipts, and the line-item data extracted from them
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and general interaction patterns within the Service
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, and IP address, collected automatically for security and performance

We do not collect precise geolocation, and we do not require payment information to use the free tier of the Service.

03Receipt & Health-Adjacent Data

Your grocery purchase history can reveal information about a celiac or gluten-sensitive diagnosis. We treat this data as sensitive and restrict internal access accordingly.

Receipt images and the gluten-free classifications derived from them are stored in your account and are visible only to you, unless you explicitly choose to share a report (for example, exporting a summary for a tax preparer). We do not use your receipt or purchase data to make inferences about your health for advertising purposes, and we do not sell this data to data brokers.

04How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service, including organizing your receipts and generating statistics
  • Identify gluten-free items, potential tax-deductible purchases, and possible overcharges within your uploaded receipts
  • Communicate with you about your account, security notices, or product updates
  • Improve the reliability and accuracy of our receipt-processing features
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity

05AI Processing

BreadWinner is designed to use automated, AI-assisted processing to read receipt images and classify line items. When this processing is active, receipt content may be sent to third-party AI processing providers under contractual terms that prohibit them from using your data to train their own models or from retaining it beyond what is needed to return a result to us.

You can review and correct any automated classification within the app. Automated results are not independently verified against store or medical records and should be treated as organizational assistance, not medical or tax determinations.

06Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

RecipientPurpose
Cloud hosting providersStoring account data and receipt images securely
AI processing providersReading and classifying uploaded receipt content
Analytics providersUnderstanding aggregate, de-identified usage trends
Legal & regulatory bodiesOnly when required by law, subpoena, or valid legal process

Any service provider we work with is bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations consistent with this Policy.

07Storage & Security

Data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using industry-standard encryption. Access to receipt content and account data within our systems is limited to personnel who need it to operate or support the Service, and is logged for auditing.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If we become aware of a security incident affecting your data, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

08Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account
  • Delete your account and associated receipt data
  • Export your receipt history and statistics in a portable format
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data

You can exercise most of these rights directly from your account settings. For anything else, contact us using the details below.

09Data Retention

We retain your account and receipt data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we permanently remove your receipt images and personal data from our production systems within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes.

10Children's Privacy

BreadWinner is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

11Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Material changes will be communicated by email or an in-app notice before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
